These 1-hit guides are designed to be used with Death Dealer + Summon Shield Imp.

-----------------

Buff and equipment preparation

1. Wear equipment shown in the 1-hit guide
2. Get basic damage buffs (Berserk, EA, EW, Fury)
2b. Alternatively enter stats and buffs on Rubicksman's old site
(stats.rubicksman.com/layout.php, stats.rubicksman.com/buffs.php)
3. Get basic attack buffs (DC, KE)
4. Get Potion of Greater Doubler (Doubler 750)
5. Get buffs Summon Shield Imp and Death Dealer
6. Ensure you have a kill streak of 60 for Death Dealer 175
. or a kill streak of 400 for Potion of Lesser Death Dealer (Death Dealer 25)

Finding out the CA level @ http://mowglik.livinloud.ca/CA.html

-----------------

Hunting

1. You need to preserve your kill streak (ie. not to get killed) to sustain Death Dealer damage bonus
2. Watch Summon Shield Imp HP's by hovering a mouse pointer over the buff icon shown in your profile
3. When there is 1 imp HP left, watch every combat so you don't get killed
4. Get new imp after the old one is knocked out

-----------------

How to interpret the hunting guides

- Bolded items represent changed items from a previous level equipment list.
- Abbreviations under the equipment list are the needed buffs.
- Stats on the top of the blue bar are the player stats originating from equipment, level up points and guild structures.
. Stats below the blue bar are the stats you need to beat a creature.
- PS represents a creature's piercing strike value.

Miscellaneous tips

Be sure to only attack creatures which match with the equipment list on the guide.
Remember to deactivate Nightmare Visage after you have completed a level, for the buff decreases your attack.
Some 2-hit levels require a DD buff (Death Dealer). You don't need Summon Shield Imp to protect your kill streak,
. as specific creatures in DD levels have no piercing strike or critical hit. An armor-based setup keeps you alive.

The DD damage bonus is in effect if you have a sufficient kill streak before the buff is activated.
The DD damage bonus is dynamic. A kill streak of 30 with DD 175 gets you a 10% damage bonus.
If you keep killing creatures, the bonus will increase by one notch every 5 kills until the 20% damage bonus is reached.
